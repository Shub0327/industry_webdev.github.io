Rupture Disk

Graphite Rupture discs are, next to safety valves, the most commonly used pressure protection devices in industrial plants. They protect process Equipment’s vessels and pipelines from deformation and other damage. Rupture disc bursts to relieve the total system from undesirable pressure Rupture disc the main objective is to optimally protect the equipment’s and, at the same time, minimise the downtime of the system
