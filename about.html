HISTORY

Corporate Legacy

In 2016, a group of technocrats established Bhumistha Infra Services (BIS), which has its manufacturing center located in Madap, Tal-Khalapur and its head office in Vashi, Navi Mumbai. BIS has established itself as a leading company in the chemical process industry, specializing in the design, manufacture, installation, and commissioning of process equipment. Their expertise in handling turnkey projects is exemplary, and their experience makes them a preferred choice.

BIS's unique strength lies in manufacturing chemical process equipment for various industries such as chemicals, bulk drugs, API, and pharmaceuticals, using unique materials such as graphite and other special MOCs. As a first-generation entrepreneur group, they are making significant progress in both national and international markets, as evident from their client list and repeat purchases.

In today's competitive market scenario, where the chemical and allied industries need turnkey project specialists with industry experience, BIS stands out with its in -depth knowledge of the processes, quick problem-solving abilities, and capacity to design, manufacture, install, commission, and provide after-sales service until the customer is fully satisfied.

WHAT WE DO

Our Role

BIS specializes in designing and manufacturing specialty equipment in polymers such as PPH, PVDF, PTFE, PFA, etc., with full vacuum and pressure testing capabilities. They also manufacture all-glass specialty static equipment, columns, and pressure-tested designed pots.

BIS's main objective is to provide turnkey projects that include detailed engineering with simulation, layouts, supervision support, practical SOPs, operation, and maintenance training, necessary documentation such as IQ, DQ, OQ, in-plant training, specifically for chemical process industries. They also offer lean six sigma project -based training tailored to clients' requirements in turnkey projects.

BIS transitioned from a process equipment supplier to a turnkey solution and system provider to eliminate the information/knowledge gap between consultants and equipment manufacturers, which often leads to system inefficiency or ineffectiveness. As a responsible supply chain member, BIS takes up the challenge of providing reliable solutions and ensuring that their equipment and system work efficiently for the client. This approach has earned them long-term associations and repeat orders from their clients.
